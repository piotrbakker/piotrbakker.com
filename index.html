I help technology companies make products people like to use. I work across the complete design process, from deep dives into complex user experience problems all the way to final implementation assets.

Advanced products need advanced interfaces. Let me help you identify what you can do better and propose a range of practical solutions.

Visual design

Projects

Over the years I’ve worked with a number of startups and established companies. Most recently, I’ve been collaborating with Wizehire.com, a recruiting platform for small business owners. Earlier, I’ve designed a data dashboard for Rainforest Connection (Google.org grant recipient), helped rebuild Mixbook.com’s photo book editor, assisted Brainly.com with the launch of their first subscription plan and gave Mytos.bio (YC ’18) a hand with their first web app. I’ve also worked on internal tooling projects at Bol.com (largest Dutch e-commerce site) and TMG.nl (Dutch/Belgian media publisher). Today I continue working with teams large and small, particularly on early-stage web and mobile apps (iOS and Android).
